Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Facebook Business Page and Facebook Ad Account?

Yes! Both of these items are necessary to run ads through Facebook and Instagram. If you don't have these set up, or don't know how to set them up, dont worry! As part of our new client set up, we walk you through all of the set up over the phone.

Do I need to come up with my own ad copy?

No! Our job is to make sure that the ad copy we use generates results. We take your input draft your ad and get final approval from you before we publish and start bringing you leads.

What can you offer that I can't do myself

A lot! Facebook Marketing is really a science. While you certainly have the ability to give it a go, we have put in the hours to make sure our ads work! Running a small business is hard and we are sure it is more cost effective to hire us to run succesful ads than for you to spend the months learning how to do it.

Are there a lot of up front costs?

No! We find the budget that works for you. Effective advertising does not have to cost an arm and a leg. We design our ads to have the greatest reach based on the budget you outline.
